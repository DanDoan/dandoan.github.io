About

Who I Am

I'm the child of refugee parents who fled a war-torn country and rebuilt their lives in America — earning advanced degrees and instilling in me a deep appreciation for hard work and the opportunities we're given.

I earned a B.S. in Computer Engineering from San Jose State University, where my team won 2nd place at the Silicon Valley Innovation Challenge with a hand interface device.

Since then I've spent over a decade in firmware and validation — progressing from individual contributor to manager across Micron, SanDisk, Apple, Cruise, Zoox, and now Wayve, where I lead an engineering team building the future of autonomous vehicles.

Outside of work I'm a real estate investor — I built custom data analysis tools on top of live property management APIs to track occupancy, lease risk, and market trends across my DFW portfolio.
